Create a premium ecommerce website for **SR Foods – Farm to Home**, a specialty food brand focused on **pickles** and **karam powders/spice blends** from Andhra-style cuisine. The website should feel **spicy, authentic, regional, bold, and appetizing**, while still looking clean, modern, and trustworthy for online shopping.

## Brand Direction
The visual language should be strongly inspired by the identity of **“Guntur Mirchi Kaaram – Spicy & Authentic.”**  
Make the Names catering to Telugu Audience, 
The overall mood should communicate:
- fiery flavor
- traditional authenticity
- homemade/farm-to-home quality
- richness of Andhra spices
- trust and freshness
- bold regional food identity

This is not a generic grocery website. It is a **specialized spice and pickle store** with a strong identity around **red chilli, karam, masala richness, and handcrafted taste**.

## Core Website Structure
Build the website with a clear **top navigation bar** that includes:
- Home
- Pickles
- Karam
- About Us
- Contact
- Cart

The **Pickles** and **Karam** sections should feel like two major product collections and should be easy to explore from the navigation bar.

## Homepage Requirements
Design a compelling homepage with the following sections:

### 1. Hero Section
Create a bold hero banner featuring:
- rich food imagery of red chilli powder, pickles, spices, traditional jars, or Andhra-style food textures
- headline such as: **“Spicy & Authentic Flavours from Farm to Home”**
- supporting text highlighting homemade taste, premium ingredients, and regional authenticity
- strong CTA buttons like:
  - Shop Pickles
  - Shop Karam

The hero section should immediately communicate that this brand is about **authentic spicy foods**, especially karam and pickles.

### 2. Featured Collections
Create two visually distinct featured collection cards:
- **Pickles Collection**
- **Karam Collection**

Each collection card should include:
- strong product imagery
- short description
- call-to-action button like “Explore Pickles” or “Explore Karam”

### 3. Brand Story / About Section
Add a section introducing **SR Foods – Farm to Home** as a brand that brings authentic traditional flavors to customers.  
Tone should feel warm, rooted, family-driven, and quality-focused.

Possible themes:
- inspired by Andhra food heritage
- made with carefully selected ingredients
- bold spice blends and traditional recipes
- crafted for people who love strong, real flavor

### 4. Best Selling Products
Add a product grid with sample featured items from both categories:
- mango pickle
- gongura pickle
- avakaya
- vellulli karam
- idli karam
- kandi podi / karam powder

Each product card should include:
- product image
- name
- short flavor description
- price
- add to cart button

### 5. Why Choose Us
Include a trust-building section with icons or highlight cards for:
- authentic recipes
- farm-fresh ingredients
- handmade taste
- hygienic preparation
- rich regional flavor
- doorstep delivery

### 6. Testimonials
Include customer reviews that emphasize:
- authentic homemade taste
- strong spice flavor
- quality packaging
- nostalgia and regional connection

### 7. Footer
Footer should include:
- logo
- quick links
- contact info
- social media links
- copyright
- newsletter signup

## Design Style
The design should feel:
- bold but premium
- spicy and warm
- modern ecommerce with traditional food identity
- clean layout with strong imagery
- appetizing and high-contrast

## Color Palette
Use colors inspired by chilli, spices, and earthy ingredients:
- deep red
- chilli red
- warm orange-red
- dark maroon
- turmeric/golden accents
- off-white / cream backgrounds
- optional dark charcoal for contrast

Avoid making it look neon or too synthetic.  
The palette should feel **natural, rich, grounded, and flavorful**.

## Typography
Use typography that feels:
- bold and confident for headings
- clean and readable for body text
- slightly rustic or culturally rich in feel, but not overly decorative

Headings should look strong and memorable, while the body text should support a professional ecommerce experience.

## UI and Layout
The site should have:
- a clean responsive layout
- strong spacing
- modern product cards
- sticky navigation
- clean hover interactions
- mobile-friendly design
- smooth browsing for ecommerce
